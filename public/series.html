Top best series that I've seen this year

  1. Netflix

    • Sorjonen

      This is not an English series, but the crime and the detective is another level. 10 out of 10. I recommend it for those interested in thriller drama.

    • The Fall

      A dark story from a serial killers perspective. Also the detective is top.

    • Unbelievable

      • Brilliant casting and acting. It's a crime drama series, based on a real life story about a girl and a rapist.

  2. HBO

    • Mare of Easttown

      A very realistic thriller drama mini-series with Kate Winslet, a skilled detective,investigating the murder of a young girl.

    • The last of us

      A really emotional story about survival and the connection between two broken souls.

    • True detectives

      • It's definetly worth watching. The series tell the story of some detectives and their dedication to solving a particular case. I really liked it.
